Clubs, Committees and Societies:

  • Cultural Society: This Society organises cultural programmes and functions for the students and encourages them to participate in it.

  • Bizarre: The Fashion Society: This Society is for the students interested in the fashion industry and helps them to boost their style sense.

  • Dictum: The Public Speaking Society: The Debating Society offers opportunities for students to logically explain their points of view and focus on one side of the argument.

  • Footloose: The Dance Society: This Society is responsible for organising all the dance events at Shivaji College, Delhi. It organises dance events in many forms.

  • Reverb: The Music Society: The members of this society organise various musical events and programmes for the students and it provides a platform to the students to showcase their musical talent.

  • Shutterbugs: The Photography Society:  This Society provides a platform for budding photographers to showcase their photography talent.

  • Vayam:  The Theatre Society: This Society is for theatre and dramatics enthusiasts.This club consists of actors and performers. It organises events like street plays, stage plays etc.

  • Vibgyor: The Fine Arts Society: This society promotes fine arts and provides a platform to the students to showcase their skills in the field of art.

  • Eco Club: The members of this Club make continuous efforts to conserve and protect the environment and connect with the society and communities at large. 

  • National Service Scheme: NSS creates social awareness and imparts human values in students and creates a spirit of social service amongst them.

  • National Cadet Corps:  NCC works with an objective to create a suitable environment to motivate the youth to take up a career in the Armed Forces.
